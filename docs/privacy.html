Quizard Privacy Policy

Effective July 24, 2026 · Quizard is founded and run by middle schoolers working toward college.

The short version

We collect as close to nothing as we can get away with. No ads, no tracking, no selling data, ever.

What we collect

  • A player name you choose (it doesn't need to be your real name — wizard names encouraged) and an optional password, stored only as a cryptographic hash.
  • Practice progress: scores, skill levels, streaks, missed questions, and settings, synced to your account so it follows you between devices.
  • That's it. No email, no real name, no location, no contacts, no photos, no advertising identifiers.

The AI Coach ("Sage")

Sage is powered by Claude, made by Anthropic. When you chat with Sage, your message and the current problem are sent to Anthropic to generate the reply, then the reply comes back. We do not store coach conversations. A parent or guardian must approve the Coach inside the app before it works, and Sage is instructed to discuss only schoolwork and never to ask for personal information.

Online features

If you use online races, leaderboards, or friends, your player name, rating, and win/loss record are visible to other players. You can hide your rating in Settings.

Payments

Purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) — we never see or store card numbers.

Deleting your data

In the app: Manage account → Delete this account removes your account and synced data from our servers. You can also open an issue at our public repository and we'll delete it for you.

Children

Quizard is built for students. We designed it to work without collecting personal information: pseudonymous names, no email, parent approval before any AI feature. If you believe a child has submitted personal information, contact us below and we will delete it.
